Chief Information Officer (CIO)
Company: Northwind Health Systems

About the role:
Northwind Health Systems is seeking an executive technology leader to serve as
Chief Information Officer. The CIO will own enterprise technology strategy,
drive digital transformation across clinical and business operations, and lead a
150+ person technology organization. This is a senior executive role reporting
to the CEO and partnering with the board.

Responsibilities:
- Define and execute a multi-year enterprise IT and digital transformation strategy.
- Lead adoption of AI and data analytics to improve clinical and operational outcomes.
- Oversee cybersecurity, risk management, and regulatory compliance (HIPAA).
- Manage a technology budget exceeding $80M and a large cross-functional team.
- Modernize legacy systems and migrate core workloads to the cloud (Azure/AWS).
- Partner with executive leadership on governance, KPIs, and board reporting.
- Build and mentor high-performing engineering, data, and security teams.

Required qualifications:
- 15+ years of progressive IT leadership, including 5+ years as a CIO, CTO, or VP.
- Proven experience leading enterprise digital transformation at scale.
- Strong background in cloud migration, cybersecurity, and data/AI strategy.
- Experience managing large budgets and vendor/partner relationships.
- Excellent executive communication and board-level presentation skills.

Preferred qualifications:
- Healthcare or regulated-industry experience.
- Familiarity with Agile delivery, ITIL, and enterprise architecture (TOGAF).
- MBA or equivalent business leadership credential.

Keywords: digital transformation, AI, cloud migration, cybersecurity, HIPAA,
enterprise architecture, IT strategy, executive leadership, data analytics,
budget management, governance, Azure, AWS.
